Discover Curitiba, Paraná, Brazil

Welcome!

Welcome to Curitiba, one of Brazil's cultural gems!

Elevation 🏔️

Curitiba is located at an elevation of approximately 945 meters above sea level.

Population 📈

Curitiba is home to over 1.9 million people.

Main Industries 🏢

Curitiba's economy thrives on sectors like:

  • Automotive manufacturing
  • Information technology
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Food processing
  • Financial services

Climate ☀️🌨️

Curitiba enjoys a subtropical highland climate with mild summers and cool winters. Temperatures typically range from:

  • Summer (December to February): 15°C to 27°C (59°F to 81°F)
  • Winter (June to August): 8°C to 22°C (46°F to 72°F)

Festivals ✨

Curitiba hosts various cultural events throughout the year, including:

  • Festival de Curitiba (Curitiba Theater Festival)
  • Coolritiba (Ecological Music Festival)
  • Winter Festival
  • Christmas Street
  • Feira do Largo da Ordem (Handicraft Market)

Culture 👥

Curitiba boasts a rich cultural heritage and is known for its:

  • Green urban planning and parks
  • High quality of life
  • Cultural diversity
  • Strong emphasis on education and sustainability
